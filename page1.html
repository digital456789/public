About PSI:

Public Speaking Institute (PSI) helps individuals, professionals to hone their skills who wish to learn public speaking, contribute to make a difference with their thoughts,stories and ideas with primary focus on public speaking, communication, personal development and leadership mindset.

PSI was founded in 2010 is an online growing community of public speakers, orators, speaker coaches to promote public speaking skills, presentation literacy,art of communication & storytelling.

We are a progressive non-profit organization and seek to provide the most effective education to every student who enters our doors in search of learning. We believe in developing a caring community through our institute, exercising concern and respect for the welfare of others and emphasizing the over-riding importance of good Human relations, education awareness based upon moral values, sensitivity, tolerance and good-will.

PSI is an online community which has now grown to one of the India's largest community on Facebook with around 2.2 lac global members having authors,youth,corporate executives,entrepreneurs,professional trainers,aspiring orators, public speakers and professional orators worldwide.

PSI offers exclusive online courses on public speaking,effective communication, body language, confidence building on the stage for students, working professionals, entrepreneurs, start-up business owners to improve their personality and speaking skills.

PSI works mainly through personal coaching & training,group coaching and online workshop based model with a team of qualified trainers and coaches worldwide to help individuals.
