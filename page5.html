REFUND AND CANCELLATION 

Generally, online training course orders are NOT eligible for refund, cancellation or transfer to a Seminar training course.We MAY approve a refund/transfer request provided the Student meets the following requirements:

The Student MUST notify us of their desire to transfer or cancel IN WRITING within 24-hours of placing the online order. Requests MUST be sent via email (VERBAL requests are NOT accepted) to following address: institutepublicspeaking@gmail.com

Other Terms and Conditions:
Online training courses may NOT be transferred to another student.
Orders placed with a “Corporate Account” or “Discount Code” are NOT eligible for a refund, cancellation or transfer.NO EXCEPTIONS.
